Standard libraries have
- Things you could not implement on your own
  - Opening a file, setting a timer
- Things so common a standard definition is appropriate.
  - Examples: List.map, string concatenation

You should get comfortable seeking out documentation and gaining
intuition on where to look.

http://www.standardml.org/Basis/manpages.html
